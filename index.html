Reimbursement Policy

The Business Travel Reimbursement Policy aims to provide clear guidelines for employees who travel on behalf of the company.
The policy ensures that travel expenses are managed effectively and reimbursed promptly, promoting fair and consistent treatment for all employees.

General Guidelines
1. Travel Approval: All business trips must be pre-approved by the relevant authorities within the company.
2. Booking: All travel arrangements should be booked through the companyâ€™s preferred travel agency, American Express Global Business Travel.
3. Receipts: Original receipts must be submitted for all expenses to be eligible for reimbursement.

Allowable Expenses
1. Accommodation: Hotel stays up to $160 per night are reimbursable. Higher rates require pre-approval from a manager.
2. Meals and Entertainment: Daily meal allowances vary based on the destination and nature of the trip.
3. Transportation: Airfare, rental cars, and other transportation costs are reimbursable.
4. Miscellaneous: Incidentals such as dry cleaning, taxis, and business services are reimbursable.

Submission Process
1. Initiate Reimbursement: Start by submitting a reimbursement request through the app.
2. Approvals: The request will go through several approval stages.
3. Documentation: Ensure all relevant receipts and justifications are attached to the request.
4. Timeline: Submit requests within 30 days of completing travel.

Non-Reimbursable Expenses
1. Personal entertainment (e.g., movies, personal travel).
2. Fines or penalties (e.g., parking tickets, traffic violations).
3. Costs incurred by companions who are not on company business.
4. Luxury items (e.g., spa treatments, high-end dining without client involvement).

Compliance
Employees must comply with this policy to ensure prompt and fair reimbursement. Failure to adhere to these guidelines may result in delayed or denied reimbursements.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions to help you understand the reimbursement process.

Q1: How do I submit a reimbursement request?
A1: You can submit a reimbursement request through the Business Travel Reimbursement App. Ensure you attach all necessary receipts and justifications.

Q2: What is the deadline for submitting reimbursement requests?
A2: Reimbursement requests must be submitted within 30 days of completing your travel.

Q3: Can I get reimbursed for personal entertainment expenses?
A3: No, personal entertainment expenses are not reimbursable.

Q4: What if my hotel costs exceed $160 per night?
A4: If your hotel costs exceed $160 per night, you must get pre-approval from a manager.
